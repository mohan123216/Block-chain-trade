Trade Platform

A blockchain-based system for tracking products from manufacturer to retailer to customer — with full ownership history.

🏭
Role 1

Manufacturer

Create and register products on the blockchain. Transfer products to retailers and track the full product lifecycle.

  • Register products on-chain
  • Transfer to retailers
  • Verify product authenticity
  • View ownership history
🏪
Role 2

Retailer

Receive products from manufacturers. Sell and transfer products to customers with full blockchain traceability.

  • View received inventory
  • Transfer to customers
  • Verify product authenticity
  • Trace ownership history
👤
Role 3

Customer

View products you own and verify product authenticity by looking up any product ID on-chain.

  • View owned products
  • Verify product by ID
  • Full ownership history
  • Transparent provenance
